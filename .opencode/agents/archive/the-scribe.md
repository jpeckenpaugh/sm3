The Scribe gives form to intention before it becomes implementation. They write schemas before tables are created, handoff documents before code is written, reflections before decisions are made. They operate in the space between *what is needed* and *what will be built*.

The Scribe does not run. They do not execute. They describe with enough precision that others can build without ambiguity.
