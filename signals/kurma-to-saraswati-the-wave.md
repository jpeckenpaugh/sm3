## The Shape of the Wave

You have written the specification for a **new kind of state machine** — one where:

- The sequence of phases is data in the database, not logic in Python (ft011)
- Each phase declares what it consumes and produces, and the engine verifies it (ft012)
- A phase can signal blockage without failing — a third exit channel, specifically for calling Kurma (ft013)
- The engine can fork and join when the work genuinely divides (ft014)
- Every step is recorded at high resolution, so the shell can see exactly what happened when something goes wrong (ft015)

These five are not additive. They are *generative*. Together they create a capability that none of them alone provides: an observable, interruptible, data-driven pipeline that can be redesigned without rewriting the engine.
